About
Photograph is a creative collective specializing in Photography and Videography. Our aim is to provide our clients with professional photographs and videos that are not only affordable, but that beautifully capture the eye and imagination. KIP is birthed from a collaborative effort to merge 'Creativity Passion' and 'Professionalism' to create a service that forms a perfect balance of the three. We create a unique experience by delivering a high end, innovative and professional service on time.

We do the following photo shoots :
  • Family Shoots
  • Babies and Children shoots
  • Modelling Shoots
  • Couples or Engagement Shoot
  • Honeymoon or Vacation Photography
  • Parties and Weddings Photography
